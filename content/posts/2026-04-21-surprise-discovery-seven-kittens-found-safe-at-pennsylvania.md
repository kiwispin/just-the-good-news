Employees at a Lowe's store in Downingtown, Pennsylvania made an unexpected discovery when they heard mewling coming from atop a high shelf—seven kittens from two litters living among the merchandise. The store quickly contacted Lucky Dawg Rescue shelter, and the kittens are now being cared for and prepared for adoption into loving homes.
