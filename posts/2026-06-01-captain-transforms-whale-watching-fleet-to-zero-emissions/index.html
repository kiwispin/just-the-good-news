Captain Transforms Whale-Watching Fleet to Zero Emissions After Five-Year Effort

Good Good Good 1 June 2026

Photo: Thomas Kelley / Unsplash

Captain Dan Salas of Harbor Breeze Cruises has successfully converted his whale-watching boat to run emission-free, eliminating pollution equivalent to a 186-mile car trip per outing. His five-year project demonstrates how the maritime industry can adopt cleaner technologies while maintaining popular tourist experiences. The initiative shows that meaningful environmental progress is possible when dedicated individuals commit to sustainable innovation.
