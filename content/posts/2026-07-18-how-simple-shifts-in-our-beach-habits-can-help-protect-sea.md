By making small adjustments to our beach routines—such as clearing sandcastles, minimizing light pollution, and packing out plastic waste—beachgoers can actively safeguard endangered sea turtle hatchlings. These straightforward actions ensure a clearer, safer path for the young turtles as they make their crucial journey to the ocean.
