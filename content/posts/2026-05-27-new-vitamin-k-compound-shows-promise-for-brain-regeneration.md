Japanese researchers have developed enhanced vitamin K-based compounds that are three times more effective at regenerating brain neurons than natural vitamin K alone. This breakthrough could reshape treatment approaches for neurodegenerative diseases like Alzheimer's and Parkinson's by harnessing the brain's own ability to heal itself.
