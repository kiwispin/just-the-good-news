Researchers have mapped the neural pathway that links deep sleep with the release of growth hormone, showing how the two actively support muscle repair, fat metabolism, and brain health. This discovery offers a clearer understanding of the restorative power of rest and opens the door to new therapies for sleep disorders and neurodegenerative conditions.
