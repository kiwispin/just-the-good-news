Researchers have discovered that the tau protein, long studied primarily for its link to Alzheimer's, actually plays a vital positive role in helping the brain organize and store new life experiences into permanent memories. This breakthrough offers scientists a much clearer roadmap for developing targeted therapies to protect and preserve cognitive health.
