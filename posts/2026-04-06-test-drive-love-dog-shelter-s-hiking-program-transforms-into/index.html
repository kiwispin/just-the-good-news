Test Drive Love: Dog Shelter's Hiking Program Transforms Shelter Stays Into Forever Homes

Good Good Good 6 April 2026

Photo: Judy Beth Morris / Unsplash

An animal shelter is removing barriers to adoption by letting potential adopters borrow dogs for outdoor adventures before making a commitment. With a quick 15-20 minute sign-up process, the program helps both local residents and visitors forge genuine connections with dogs on the trail—connections that frequently lead to adoption.
