Nutella, a sea turtle rescued from fishing line entanglement in Key Largo last December, has made a full recovery thanks to the dedicated care of Florida's Turtle Hospital. After four months of treatment for tumors and injuries, she was released back into the ocean, giving hope to wildlife rehabilitation efforts across the state.
