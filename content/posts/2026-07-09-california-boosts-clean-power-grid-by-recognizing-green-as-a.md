Governor Gavin Newsom has signed SB 1350 into law, officially classifying electricity generated from green hydrogen as a renewable energy source under California's Renewables Portfolio Standard. This legislative milestone allows power plants utilizing green hydrogen to access key financial incentives, accelerating the state's transition toward a fully clean energy grid.
