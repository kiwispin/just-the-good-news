Using modern sonar technology, researchers have successfully located a vibrant deep-water coral reef off the coast of West Africa that was presumed dead for over half a century. The newly mapped ecosystem is teeming with marine life, offering scientists a hopeful new opportunity to study and protect these resilient deep-sea habitats.
