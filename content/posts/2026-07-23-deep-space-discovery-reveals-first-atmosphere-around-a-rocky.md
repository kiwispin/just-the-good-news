Astronomers have detected a confirmed atmosphere around the rocky exoplanet LHS 1140 b, which sits in the habitable zone of a star 48 light-years away. This historic discovery offers a promising new target in the search for life, as the planet appears to have preserved its atmosphere for billions of years.
