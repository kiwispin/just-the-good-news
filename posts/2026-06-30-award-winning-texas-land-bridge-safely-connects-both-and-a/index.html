Award-Winning Texas Land Bridge Safely Connects Both Wildlife and People Across a Busy Six-Lane Highway

Good Good Good 30 June 2026

Photo: Ana Lucía Licea / Unsplash

The Robert L.B. Tobin Land Bridge offers a pioneering solution to habitat fragmentation by providing a lush, safe passage for both native wildlife and pedestrians. This first-of-its-kind overpass has earned prestigious design awards for successfully balancing the needs of the local community with the preservation of natural ecosystems.
