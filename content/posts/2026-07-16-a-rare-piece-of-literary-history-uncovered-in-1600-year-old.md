Archaeologists have made a historic find after discovering a fragment of Homer's Iliad preserved inside an ancient Egyptian mummy. This unique artifact marks the first known instance of a Greek literary text being intentionally included in the mummification process, offering researchers a beautiful new connection between two iconic ancient cultures.
