New Painless One-Hour Brush Test Could Revolutionize Oral Cancer Detection

The Optimist Daily 22 July 2026

Photo: Alex Padurariu / Unsplash

Researchers from the Queen Mary University of London have successfully trialed a simple, non-invasive mouth swab that detects oral cancer in under an hour. By analyzing just four key genes, this painless method achieves the same diagnostic accuracy as a traditional surgical biopsy without the need for a scalpel.
