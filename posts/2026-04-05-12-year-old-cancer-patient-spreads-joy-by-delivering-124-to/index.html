12-Year-Old Cancer Patient Spreads Joy by Delivering 124 Easter Baskets to Hospitalized Children

Good News Network 5 April 2026

Photo: Ashley West Edwards / Unsplash

Nathan Yuill, a 12-year-old undergoing treatment for stage-4 non-Hodgkin lymphoma, raised $2,000 to create Easter baskets for nearly every child at Providence Children's Hospital. With his own remission in sight, Nathan chose to brighten the days of fellow young patients facing their own health challenges.
