How Listening to Women's Real-Life Experiences Led Illinois to Tackle the Menopause Care Gap

The Optimist Daily 14 July 2026

Photo: alex starnes / Unsplash

During statewide financial wellness tours, Illinois Lt. Gov. Juliana Stratton discovered that menopause was a major, unspoken barrier to women's economic and professional well-being. By actively listening to these community insights, the state is now spearheading practical solutions to better support women's health and careers.
