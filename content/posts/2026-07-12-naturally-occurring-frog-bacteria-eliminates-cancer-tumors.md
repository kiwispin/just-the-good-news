Researchers in Japan have discovered that a natural bacteria found in the gut of a Japanese tree frog possesses highly potent anticancer properties. Led by Professor Eijiro Miyako, the scientific team observed that a single dose of the bacteria successfully destroyed cancer tumors, opening an exciting new pathway for targeted oncology treatments.
