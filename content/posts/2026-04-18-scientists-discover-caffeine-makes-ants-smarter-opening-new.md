Researchers found that caffeine sharpens ants' learning abilities, helping invasive Argentine ants navigate more efficiently and reduce travel time by up to 38%. The discovery offers a promising new approach to making pest control baits significantly more effective, potentially reducing the need for harsh chemical pesticides.
