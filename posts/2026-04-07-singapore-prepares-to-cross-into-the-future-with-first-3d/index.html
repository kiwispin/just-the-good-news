Singapore Prepares to Cross Into the Future With First 3D-Printed Bridge

Good News Network 7 April 2026

Photo: Kelsey He / Unsplash

Singapore's transportation officials are planning to debut innovative 3D-printed concrete technology with a new 30-foot pedestrian bridge crossing a waterway by 2028. The bridge, part of a larger initiative to enhance transit connectivity in the Jurong River and Temah areas, represents the city-state's first major infrastructure project using this cutting-edge construction method after thorough testing and validation.
