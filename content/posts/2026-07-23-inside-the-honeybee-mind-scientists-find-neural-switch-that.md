Researchers have discovered specific brain circuits in honeybees that dictate their roles within the hive as they age. By gently adjusting these neural switches, scientists successfully encouraged older bees to return to queen-care duties, revealing a fascinating and flexible blueprint behind the colony's natural teamwork.
