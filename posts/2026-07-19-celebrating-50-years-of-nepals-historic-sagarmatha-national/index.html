Celebrating 50 Years of Nepal’s Historic Sagarmatha National Park

Good News Network 19 July 2026

Photo: Sebastian Pena Lambarri / Unsplash

Five decades ago, Nepal established its very first national park, protecting over 440 square miles of pristine Himalayan landscape including the iconic Mount Everest. This milestone marked a major step forward for global conservation, preserving rich biodiversity and stunning alpine ecosystems for generations to come.
