Scientists have successfully used sugar-coated nanoparticles to bypass the blood-brain barrier and deliver tumor-suppressing instructions directly to glioblastoma cells. In laboratory studies, this novel therapy shrank tumors and boosted survival rates by 50% without causing damage to surrounding organs.
