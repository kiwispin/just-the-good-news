summary: "Scientists discovered that drawing big, staring eyes on takeaway boxes makes seagulls think something is watching them—and they get too scared to steal your food! This simple trick works because gulls are cautious birds that don't like being spotted, so they fly away to find an easier snack instead. Now you might never lose another chip to a sneaky seagull again!"
