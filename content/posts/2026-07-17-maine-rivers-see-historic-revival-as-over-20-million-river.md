Following the removal of key dams along the Penobscot River, Maine’s river herring population has staged an extraordinary recovery, with this year's annual run marking the highest count in decades. This ecological milestone marks a triumphant return for a species that was illegal to harvest just over a decade ago, showcasing the powerful impact of river restoration.
