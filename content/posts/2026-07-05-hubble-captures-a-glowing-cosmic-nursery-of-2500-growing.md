NASA's Hubble Space Telescope has revealed a stunning, detailed view of LH 95, a stellar nursery where thousands of young stars are actively growing side by side. This breakthrough observation shows that developing stars can gather life-giving cosmic dust for millions of years longer than previously thought, offering scientists exciting new insights into how stellar families form.
