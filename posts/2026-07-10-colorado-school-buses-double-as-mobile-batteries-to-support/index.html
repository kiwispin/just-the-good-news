Colorado School Buses Double as Mobile Batteries to Support the Local Power Grid

Good News Network 10 July 2026

Photo: Richard Lu / Unsplash

A new fleet of six electric school buses in the Denver area is doing double duty by transporting students and serving as backup power reservoirs. By charging overnight and feeding electricity back into the grid during peak daytime demand, the buses help stabilize the local energy infrastructure while sitting idle.
