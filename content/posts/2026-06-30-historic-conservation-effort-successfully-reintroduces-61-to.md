In a major victory for African wildlife conservation, a massive relocation project has successfully brought 61 rhinos, including a recent group of nine female white rhinos, back to Mozambique's southern national parks. This monumental effort aims to restore the country's biodiversity and rebuild animal populations that were nearly entirely lost during past decades of conflict.
