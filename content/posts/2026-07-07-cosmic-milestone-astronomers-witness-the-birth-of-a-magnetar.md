By analyzing a unique signal from a distant supernova, scientists have captured the creation of an incredibly magnetic neutron star, confirming a long-held theory about how the universe's brightest explosions are powered. This breakthrough also marks the first successful use of Einstein's general relativity to explain the inner mechanics of a supernova, opening an exciting new chapter in space exploration.
