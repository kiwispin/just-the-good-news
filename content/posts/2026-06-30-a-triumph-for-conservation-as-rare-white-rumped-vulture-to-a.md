For the first time in ten years, the critically endangered white-rumped vulture has been spotted in Cambodia's Lomphat Wildlife Sanctuary. The bird's return follows dedicated conservation efforts to restore its habitat, secure safe food sources, and eliminate the poisoning risks that previously devastated the local population.
