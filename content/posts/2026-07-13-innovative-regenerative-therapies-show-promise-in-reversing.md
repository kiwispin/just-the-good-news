Researchers in Colorado have developed a suite of therapies, including a single joint injection and a biomaterial repair kit, that prompt damaged joints to heal themselves. In animal studies, these treatments successfully recruited the body's own cells to repair cartilage, reversing osteoarthritis damage within just a few months.
