A bird bander's casual photograph shared on iNaturalist has led to the rediscovery of Ptilotus senarius, an Australian plant missing for nearly 60 years. The find highlights how everyday people with smartphones are becoming invaluable partners in scientific discovery, helping researchers track and protect rare species.
