Researchers have discovered a new species of spider in the Himalayas that shares the iconic smile-like markings of Hawaii's famous happy-face spider. DNA testing reveals this delightful trait evolved independently in both distant regions, opening up exciting new avenues for understanding how these unique creatures adapt to their environments.
