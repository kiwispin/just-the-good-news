Researchers in South Korea have developed a breakthrough system that uses high-temperature plasma to instantly convert moisture-rich coffee waste into coal-grade biochar. By eliminating the need for a lengthy pre-drying process, this rapid technology offers an efficient way to divert organic waste from landfills and generate clean, renewable energy.
