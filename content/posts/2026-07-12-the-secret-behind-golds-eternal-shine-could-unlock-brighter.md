Scientists have discovered that gold naturally self-reorganizes its surface atoms to block oxygen, explaining why the precious metal never tarnishes. This remarkable self-defense mechanism could now help researchers develop highly efficient gold-based catalysts for cleaner energy and advanced manufacturing.
