Mystery Snake Found That Looks Like Two Species Mixed Together

Wednesday 22 April 2026 •ScienceDaily

Photo: Joshua J. Cotten / Unsplash

Scientists in Myanmar discovered a brand new snake that's so unusual it looks like it can't make up its mind—sometimes it looks like one type of pit viper, sometimes another, and sometimes like something completely different! At first, they thought it must be a mix of two species, but DNA testing showed it's actually its very own special species that's never been seen before. Imagine if you woke up one day looking like both your parents at the same time—that's how confusing this snake is to scientists!
