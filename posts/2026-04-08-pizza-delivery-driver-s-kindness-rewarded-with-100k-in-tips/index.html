Pizza Delivery Driver's Kindness Rewarded With $100K in Tips

Good News Network 8 April 2026

Photo: Pauline Iakovleva / Unsplash

A 70-year-old Domino's delivery driver in Idaho received over $100,000 in tips from generous donors after his careful, conscientious work inspired a customer to start a fundraiser. What began as a $24,000 GoFundMe campaign to help the driver retire soon snowballed into overwhelming community support, reflecting how people recognize and appreciate genuine dedication to service.
