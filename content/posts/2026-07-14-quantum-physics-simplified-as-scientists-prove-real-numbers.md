Physicists have successfully demonstrated that quantum mechanics can be formulated using ordinary real numbers rather than complex imaginary ones, simplifying our fundamental understanding of the universe. This groundbreaking discovery, highlighted by the American Physical Society, opens up clearer pathways for future research in quantum computing and advanced technologies.
