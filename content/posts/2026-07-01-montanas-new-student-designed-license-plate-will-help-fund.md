A beautiful new Montana license plate, illustrated by a recent high school graduate, is expected to raise $100,000 to construct safe pathways for migrating wildlife. Funds generated from the plate sales will directly support the state's efforts to reduce animal-vehicle collisions and protect local biodiversity.
