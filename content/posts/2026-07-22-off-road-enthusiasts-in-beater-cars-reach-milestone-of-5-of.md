Participants in the annual Gambler 500 off-road rally have hit a major environmental milestone, clearing millions of pounds of illegally dumped waste from public forests and deserts. Combining a love for cheap cars, camping, and conservation, the grassroots group turns trash collection into a competitive, community-driven adventure. Their efforts have successfully restored vast acres of natural habitat across the United States.
