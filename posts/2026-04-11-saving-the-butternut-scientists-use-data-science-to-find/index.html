Saving the Butternut: Scientists Use Data Science to Find Disease-Resistant Trees

Good News Network 11 April 2026

Photo: Sebastian Jacobsen / Unsplash

The butternut tree, a valued North American species facing extinction from disease, has a new chance at survival. Virginia Tech researchers are using climate mapping and modern data science to identify trees that have naturally resisted the deadly fungus, creating a roadmap for successful reforestation efforts.
