Stolen Picasso Masterpiece Depicting Artist’s 'Golden Muse' Safely Recovered in Paris

Good News Network 6 July 2026

Photo: Pauline Loroy / Unsplash

A significant piece of art history has been successfully recovered after police in Paris located a stolen Pablo Picasso portrait valued at €12 million. The recovered painting depicts Marie-Thérèse Walter, the artist's famous 'golden muse' who inspired one of the most prolific periods of his career.
