Parents Unite: Climate Dads Find Creative Way to Tackle Environmental Action Together

Good Good Good 5 April 2026

Photo: Daria Trofimova / Unsplash

A climate activist has organized 'playdates' that allow parents to engage in environmental mobilization while their children play together, removing the common barrier of childcare. This innovative approach brings fathers together to explore their role in creating a more sustainable future, making climate action more accessible and inclusive for busy families.
