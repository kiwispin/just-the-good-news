About Just The Good News

Our Mission

Just The Good News is a nonprofit, mission-driven website that aggregates and presents only positive, uplifting news from credible sources across the English-speaking world.

We believe that the relentless negativity of mainstream media is a choice — not a reflection of reality. Good things happen every day. Scientists make breakthroughs. Communities come together. Species recover. People are kind. We’re here to surface those stories.

How It Works

Our content pipeline runs twice daily and:

  1. Fetches articles from established positive news sources via RSS
  2. Scores each article using AI to ensure it represents genuinely good news (not spin or silver linings)
  3. Summarises qualifying articles into short, engaging summaries
  4. Publishes them here within minutes — no human intervention needed

All content is AI-curated and AI-summarised. We are not a primary news source. Every article links directly to its original source.

Our Sources

We pull from dedicated positive news outlets including Positive.News, Good News Network, Reasons to be Cheerful, and others — as well as curated sections from major publications.

Our Principles

  • Curated positivity: Every article must represent genuine good news
  • Attribution first: All content links back to the original source
  • No tracking: We use privacy-friendly analytics only
  • No dark patterns: No popups, no aggressive CTAs, no doom

Contact

Found a great story we missed? Spotted an error? Get in touch: hello@justthegoodnews.org
