Luxury Resort in French Polynesia Shows How Tourism Can Save Sea Turtles

Good Good Good 20 April 2026

Photo: Kris Kispert / Unsplash

The Brando resort demonstrates that high-end tourism and marine conservation can work together effectively. Through science-driven practices, the resort has protected over 120,000 sea turtle hatchlings, proving that sustainable hospitality models can restore ecosystems while welcoming visitors.
