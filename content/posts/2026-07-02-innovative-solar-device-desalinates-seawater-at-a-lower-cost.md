A team of materials scientists in China has developed a highly efficient solar-powered device using durable, reflective nanomaterials and organic polymers to absorb over 90% of sunlight for water purification. This breakthrough technology offers a highly cost-effective and sustainable way to produce clean freshwater from the ocean. This advancement could significantly improve global access to drinking water while reducing reliance on plastic bottled water.
