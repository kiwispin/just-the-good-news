On this day in 1990, Luciano Pavarotti, Plácido Domingo, and José Carreras performed together for the very first time at the historic Baths of Caracalla in Rome. Watched by an estimated 800 million people worldwide, the legendary concert went on to produce the best-selling classical album of all time and introduced operatic music to a vast global audience.
