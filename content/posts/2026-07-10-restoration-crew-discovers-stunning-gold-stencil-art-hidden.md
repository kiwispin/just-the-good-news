While washing a high ceiling arch during renovations at St. Mary’s Basilica in Minneapolis, workers uncovered beautiful, gleaming gold stencil work hidden beneath layers of dull paint. The historic artwork, which had been obscured since at least the 1950s, restores a brilliant piece of craftsmanship to America's oldest basilica.
