Two loggerhead sea turtles, Swim Shady and Paddles, have made an incredible recovery after being rescued near Juno Beach, Florida with critical injuries from a boat strike. After four months of dedicated care, Swim Shady has healed enough to return to the ocean, demonstrating the power of wildlife rehabilitation and second chances.
