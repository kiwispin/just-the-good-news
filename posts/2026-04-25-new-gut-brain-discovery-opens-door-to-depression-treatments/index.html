New Gut-Brain Discovery Opens Door to Depression Treatments Beyond Traditional Medications

ScienceDaily 25 April 2026

Photo: little plant / Unsplash

Harvard researchers have uncovered how a common gut bacterium may contribute to depression by triggering inflammation in the body. This breakthrough understanding of the gut-brain connection could lead to innovative treatments that work through the immune system, offering new hope for people struggling with depression.
